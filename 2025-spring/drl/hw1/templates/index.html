Instructions
  1. Enter a grid size (3-9) and click Generate (HW1-1).
  2. Click on cells to set:
    • First click: Start position (green)
    • Second click: End position (red)
    • Next N-2 clicks: Obstacles (gray)
  3. Click CALC to calculate the optimal policy.
  4. Click GO to watch the robot follow the optimal path.
  5. Click RANDOM to generate a random grid and calculate the optimal policy.
  6. Click RESET to clear the grid and start over.
